Target Audience

The target audience for this website are those who are looking to invest in the local area, either by joining this group of businesses, or by becoming a customer of them.

Personas

A young adult male who achieved the highest public education. He has been a blacksmith apprentice, but is looking to decide where to set down for their journeyman life. They will view the site as a way to decide where they will settle down, comparing different rates of engagement with other nearby towns. They will likely access the website through a tablet-sized device while they're walking to and from work. They don't go online often.

An older grandmother who enjoys taking her grandchildren to the annual festival, is looking for details on upcoming dates for the next upcoming festival and wants to know what events she can plan to attend.

Scenarios

  • Who do I contact to address concerns about a local business?
  • How successful has business been in this area?
  • What's a cool small business that I could check out?
  • What are some tips for creating a business of my own?
  • Are there any updates to upcoming events I need to be aware of?
